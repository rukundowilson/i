Hi, I'm Wilson Rukundo! Crafting secure privacy solutions in the heart of Kigali City.

From the moment I could hold a pencil, I've been creating. At just 6 years old, my journey into programming began. Only two weeks after my mom brought home a brand-new HP Elitebook 8460p, I had already taught myself HTML and was writing my first program. Innovation has been my passion ever since.

Drawing has always been my first love, even more than computers. Surprisingly, it shares many similarities with coding: both require a keen eye for detail, a sense of color and balance, and the patience to correct mistakes—what coders call 'bugs.' This artistic perspective has fueled my passion for coding, making every line of code a brushstroke in my digital masterpiece.

Solving puzzles and tackling mind games like jigsaw puzzles, word games, logic challenges, crosswords, and Sudoku has always been my comfort zone. I vividly remember being 4 years old, unable to put down my mom's cell phone because it had a Sudoku game I loved. Even then, problem-solving was my passion, and it has driven me ever since.

I am currently a second-year Computer Science major at the University of Rwanda, Nyarugenge Campus. I’ve proudly graduated from Harvard's CS50x course offered by the edX platform. Equipped with a solid foundation and a passion for innovation.

I'm ready to embark on a career in software engineering. If your team needs a dedicated and skilled developer to drive meaningful change, look no further—I'm your ideal candidate.
